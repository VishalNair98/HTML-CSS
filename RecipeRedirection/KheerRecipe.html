Seviyaan kheer is also a good option for a potluck dinner during festivals or get-together.
It can be made in big quantities.
It can also be prepared a day in advance and refrigerated.
I had made this kheer during our Diwali potluck dinner with our friends here at Sydney.
It was enjoyed by all.


Ingredients for this tasty dessert

  • 2 cup vermicell
  • 2 l full fat milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1.5 tbsp ghee
  • 2 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp saffron strands
  • 1/4 cup almonds blanched
  • 1 tbsp pistachio blanched
  1. Boil full fat milk.
  2. Roast the vermicelli for about 5 minutes
  3. Add the roasted vermicelli to the boiling milk in low-steam
  4. Add 3 cups of sugar to it.
  5. Roast some nuts if needed.
  6. Mix the roasted nuts and let it be cooked for 10 minutes
  7. Tasty seviyan kheer is ready to serve.
